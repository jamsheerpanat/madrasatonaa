# Madrasatonaa V1 Scope

## Included
- School Day Timeline (today-first)
- Teacher: Your Day (strict learning flow)
- Student: My Learning Today (read-only)
- Parent: My Child’s Day (reassurance-first)
- Principal: School Rhythm Today (patterns + drill-down)
- Announcements & Academic Calendar
- Manual Fees & Receipts (non-intrusive)

## Explicitly Excluded
- Exams & marks
- Homework submission
- Chat (teacher–parent)
- Rankings or analytics
- Transport, health, vaccination
